# Notification and notice

Notification and notice are used to convey infomation about updates and required
action to parties in the Flexibility Information System (FIS). They are sent by
FIS to the relevant parties.

## Notification

Notifications are messages issued by FIS to relevant parties that inform about
events that have happened in the system.

The notification is cleared when the notification is aknowledged.

EXAMPLE - SP receives notification of suspension - illustrate that SP gets information
that they need about an event that has happened in FIS.

## Notice

Notices are messages issued by FIS to relevant parties that require action
from the recipitent.

The notice is cleared when the required task is completed.

EXAMPLE - SPG grid preq status requested - illustrate that SO gets a task they
need to complete
